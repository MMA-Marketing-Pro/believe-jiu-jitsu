Uniform Policy

Clean kimono. Clean mat.

Simple rules that keep the academy uniform, safe, and respectful for everyone on the mat.

Gi & No-Gi Dress Code

  • Gi: Black or white Gi only during basics, fundamentals, and advanced classes. Any color welcome at Open Mat.
  • No-Gi: Black shorts and rash guard. Spats or compression pants strongly recommended to reduce skin irritation and infection risk. Males: wear shorts over leg compressions.
  • Brand: You don't have to buy our brand — just keep uniforms free of other academy logos.
  • Hygiene: Wash uniforms before and after every training session, especially if you're training twice a day.
  • Rash guard: Always worn under the Gi kimono.
