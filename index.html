06 / 11 · Uniform Policy

Clean kimono.
Clean mat.

Simple rules that keep the academy uniform, safe, and respectful for everyone rolling.

  • GiBlack or white only during basics, fundamentals, and advanced classes.
  • No-GiBlack shorts + rash guard. Spats or compression pants strongly recommended.
  • BrandYou don't have to buy our brand. Just keep the kimono free of other academy logos.
  • HygieneWash before and after every session — especially if you train twice a day.
  • Rash GuardAlways worn under the Gi kimono. Always.
  • Open MatAny color Gi or No-Gi welcome. Saturday 3:00 PM.
