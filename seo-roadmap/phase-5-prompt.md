## Pricing Page Guidance

**Before starting Phase 5, ask the owner:**
1. Can we publish actual pricing? (yes/no)
2. If yes — what's the monthly membership cost, family discount, and any initiation fee?
3. What's included in the free trial? (1 class, 1 week, etc.)
4. Any current promotions?

If owner won't publish prices, Pages 1 and 2 above pivot to "value pages" that explain what's included and why it's worth it, without numbers. Pages 3, 4, 5, 6 stay informational — they discuss market ranges generically ("$150-$250/month is typical in the Charleston area").

**Required sections for the main `/pricing.html`:**
1. Breadcrumb
2. H1: "Believe Jiu Jitsu — Membership &amp; Pricing"
3. Free trial callout at the top
4. Pricing tiers (if publishing): 1-class drop-in, monthly unlimited, family plan, kids
5. What's included — uniform, open mat access, promotions, competitions
6. What's NOT included (be transparent)
7. Cancellation policy summary (link to terms)
8. FAQ (5 Qs — "is there a contract?", "can I freeze my membership?", etc.)
9. CTA — book free trial (no purchase required to start)

**Schema:** `Product` + `Offer` (if publishing prices) + `FAQPage` + `BreadcrumbList`.

## Parent Guides Template

Informational, empathetic, no fluff. These are for parents in the evaluation phase.

**Structure:**
1. Breadcrumb
2. H1
3. Lead paragraph addressing the parent's concern directly
4. Bulleted checklist OR 3-5 numbered sections
5. Callout box: "How we handle this at Believe Jiu Jitsu" — tie the content back to a specific academy practice
6. CTA to book a trial class for the kid
7. Cross-link to 2 other parent guides + 1 kids persona page + 1 kids location page

**Schema:** `Article` + `FAQPage` + `BreadcrumbList`.
